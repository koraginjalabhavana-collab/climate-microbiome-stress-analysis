This folder outlines the experimental design and analysis workflow for the project.

## Study Objective
To investigate how different climate-related stressors reshape microbial community structure across marine and terrestrial ecosystems using publicly available microbiome datasets.

## Study Design

Two ecosystems will be analyzed:

• Coral reef microbiome (marine ecosystem) under thermal stress  
• Soil microbiome (terrestrial ecosystem) under climate manipulation

## Analytical Workflow

1. Identification of suitable public microbiome datasets  
2. Data preprocessing and formatting  
3. Alpha diversity analysis  
4. Beta diversity analysis (Bray–Curtis distance)  
5. Taxonomic composition comparison  
6. Ecological interpretation of microbial strategy shifts  
7. Cross-ecosystem comparison

## Tools

The analysis will be conducted using:

- R
- RStudio
- phyloseq
- vegan
- ggplot2

## Data Sources

Public microbiome datasets will be obtained from open repositories such as:

- Earth Microbiome Project
- NCBI Sequence Read Archive
- MG-RAST
